A music blog for the people


jaggedjamboxx was conceived with the desire to part from the conventional concept of a "music blog" and become part of a bigger community through social media to allow the listener to effortlessly consume each and every delicious sound byte. This channel will focus mainly on electronic, hip-hop, funk, soul, disco and house grooves that you can dance to, but will also deliver it's fair share of jazz, alternative, and... really whatever else I consider to be "good music".
